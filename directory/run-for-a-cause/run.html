Have you ever heard of Tic Douloureux?

Me neither. Then I did hear about it, and I definitely didn't like what I was hearing. Trigeminal neuralgia , or Tic Douloureuxis , is a chronic pain condition that affects the trigeminal nerve, which carries sensation from your face to your brain

A pain often described as being stabbed in the face with an icepick.

These types of conditions can heavily impact an individuals life, providing life-long challenges that are seemingly insurmountable. That's what this page is for; simply my fuel to spread awareness of these atrotious sicknesses.




If you or a loved one suffers from something like this, let's do our part in fighting it together.
