In the topology diagram, notice that router R1 now has a Loopback interface. In this task you will be advertising that loopback into EIGRP, and then manipulating the EIGRP distance on Router-4 so that it load-balances traffic to this new network across both the Serial and FastEthernet interfaces of router R4.

Note:

If you are doing this lab on your own equipment, ensure that the Bandwidth or clockrate of your Serial interface on R4 connecting to R1 is set to 128 Kbps.
